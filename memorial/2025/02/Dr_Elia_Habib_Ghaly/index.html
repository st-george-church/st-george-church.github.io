40th Day Commemoration of the Late Dr. Elia Habib Ghaly

With faith in the resurrection and hope in eternal life, we commemorate the 40th day of the passing of our beloved Dr. Elia Habib Ghaly, who has departed in the hope of the heavenly reward.

The memorial service will take place on Sunday February 16, 2025, at Maddaugh Elementary School, as we gather in prayer and remembrance, entrusting his soul into the hands of our Lord.

Dr. Elia Habib Ghaly was a devoted servant, a loving father to Hanaa Elia Haddad, a cherished father-in-law to Tarek Haddad, and a beloved grandfather to Daniel and Marie Haddad. His legacy of faith, kindness, and wisdom will forever remain in our hearts.

May God repose his soul in the paradise of joy and grant comfort to his family and loved ones.
