Passing of
Mrs. Samia Saad Fazary

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we extend our deepest condolences to Nancy Bedaba on the passing of her beloved mother, Mrs. Samia Saad Fazary.

With deep sorrow, we announce the peaceful passing of Mrs. Samia Saad Fazary on the evening of August 7, 2025, in Naga Hammadi, Egypt. Her life was a radiant example of compassion, generosity, and unwavering love. She touched countless hearts with her grace and selflessness, leaving behind a legacy that will continue to inspire all who knew her.

Mrs. Samia Saad Fazary is survived by her devoted husband, Atef Adly Bedaba; her cherished children: Nancy, wife of Ibrahim Ibrahim; Reham, wife of Zakaria Atta; Youstina; and her son Bishoy. She was a proud and adoring grandmother to Maria and Joy Ibrahim, and Georgeno and Rina Zakaria.

The family will receive condolences on Sunday, August 17, 2025, following the Divine Liturgy at Maddaugh Elementary.
