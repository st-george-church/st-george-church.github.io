Passing of Mr. Nadi Morkos

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we extend our deepest condolences to Magdy Morkos Meshreky on the passing of his beloved brother, Mr. Nadi Morkos.

Mr. Nadi Morkos departed peacefully on June 9, 2025 in the USA, leaving behind a legacy of kindness, generosity, and a heart that gave unconditionally to all who knew him. His life was a testament to love and service, and he will be dearly missed.

May God grant comfort and strength to Magdy Morkos Meshreky, his wife Samia, children - Michael and Maria - and the entire family during this time of sorrow. May He welcome the soul of Mr. Nadi Morkos into His eternal kingdom and grant him everlasting peace.

The family will receive condolences on Sunday, June 15, 2025, following the Sunday liturgy.
