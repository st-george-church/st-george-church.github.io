Passing of
Mrs. Linda Wahib Israfanous

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we extend our deepest condolences to Nermin Abdelmalak on the passing of her beloved mother, Mrs. Linda Wahib Israfanous.

With profound sadness, we share the peaceful passing of Mrs. Linda Wahib Israfanous on November 3, 2025, in Egypt. Her life was a shining testament to compassion, generosity, and unconditional love. She touched countless hearts with her grace and selflessness, leaving behind a legacy of kindness and inspiration that will endure in the lives of all who knew her.

Mrs. Linda Wahib Israfanous, beloved wife of the late Gaballah Abdelmalak, is lovingly remembered by her devoted daughter, Nermin Abdelmalak (Ayman Morkous), and her cherished grandchildren: Jomana, Youssef, and George Morkous.

The family will graciously receive condolences on Sunday, November 9, 2025, following the Divine Liturgy at Maddaugh Elementary School.
