Passing of
Mrs. Emeli Rizkallah Yacoub

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we extend our heartfelt condolences to the family of Mrs. Emeli Rizkallah Yacoub on her passing. We pray that the Lord grants her eternal rest and comforts her family and loved ones during this difficult time.

With deep sorrow, we announce the peaceful passing of Mrs. Emeli Rizkallah Yacoub on the morning of November 13, 2025, in Port Coquitlam, BC.

Emeli was a devout follower of Christ and a shining example of Christian faith. Her life left an indelible mark on the hearts of countless individuals. She was loyal, loving, generous, compassionate, and above all, humble. Though she did not have children of her own, she was a mother to many - a mentor, confidant, and source of guidance and prayer for her siblings, goddaughters, and all who leaned on her for support.

Mrs. Emeli Rizkallah Yacoub is survived by:
  • Sisters: Leila Chammaa (Hani Chammaa), Nadia Yacoub, Ghada Malek (Tadros Malek)
  • Brother: Yousef Yacoub (Iqbal Yacoub)
  • Nephew: Ramsey Chammaa
  • Nieces: Roula Chammaa, Gina Mogannam, Dina Vasiliou, Andrea Nasmeh, Serena Yacoub, Sarah Newburg, Rania Malek
