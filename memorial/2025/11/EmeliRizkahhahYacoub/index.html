Passing of
Mrs. Samia Saad Fazary

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we extend our deepest condolences to the family of Mrs.Emeli Rizkahhah Yacoub on her passing. We pray that the Lord grants her eternal rest and that He comforts her family and loved ones during this difficult time.

With deep sorrow , we announce the peaceful passing of Mrs. Emeli Rizkallah Yacoub on the morning of Nov 13 2025, in Port Coquitlam, BC.

Emeli, a devout follower of Christ and example of Christianity, imprinted in the hearts of countless individuals. She was loyal, loving, generous, compassionate and most of all humble. She didn't have children of her own, but she was a Mother of many, a mentor and confidant for her siblings, goddaughters and anyone who leaned on her for guidance and prayers.

Mrs. Samia Saad Fazary is survived by her devoted husband, Atef Adly Bedaba; her cherished children: Nancy, wife of Ibrahim Ibrahim; Reham, wife of Zakaria Atta; Youstina; and her son Bishoy. She was a proud and adoring grandmother to Maria and Joy Ibrahim, and Georgeno and Rina Zakaria.

The family will receive condolences on Sunday, August 17, 2025, following the Divine Liturgy at Maddaugh Elementary.
