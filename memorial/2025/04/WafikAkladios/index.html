Passing of
Mr. Wafik Akladios

It is with profound sadness that we announce the passing of Mr. Wafik Akladios on March 31st 2025 in Surrey, BC

Wafik is survived by his son Tamer (Daniela), daughter Tamirane (Michael) and grandchildren Arthanious, Damian and Anneliese.

He is also survived by his brother Reda (Fernanda) their daughter Katherine and several cousins in Egypt and Canada.

The funeral service will take place on Wednesday, April 9th, 2025, at 12:00 PM (noon) at St. Mary Coptic Orthodox Church, located at 12469 104th Avenue, Surrey. Following the service, a burial will be held at Victory Memorial, 14831 28th Avenue, Surrey.
