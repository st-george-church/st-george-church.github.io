In Loving Memory

Mr. Wafik Akladios

On behalf of HG Bishop Mina, Hegumen Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we extend our heartfelt condolences to the family of Mr. Wafik Akladios during this difficult time. Please know that our thoughts and prayers are with you as we mourn his passing.

It is with profound sadness that we announce the passing of Mr. Wafik Akladios on March 31st, 2025 in Surrey, BC.

Wafik was preceded by his late wife Mona Gad and is survived by his son Tamer (Daniela), daughter Tamirane (Michael) and grandchildren Arthanious, Damian and Anneliese.

He is also survived by his brother Reda (Fernanda), their daughter Katherine, and several cousins in Egypt and Canada.
