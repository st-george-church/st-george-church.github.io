Passing of
Mrs. Mary Bogdady

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we extend our heartfelt condolences to Tarek Haddad and Nermine Abadir on the passing of their beloved aunt, Mrs. Mary Bogdady. May the Lord grant her eternal rest and surround her family and all who mourn her with comfort and strength.

A life shaped by steadfast devotion to Christ, she reflected the depth and sincerity of true Christian faith in every step she took. Her influence reached countless people, leaving an impact that will endure. Loyal, loving, generous, and profoundly compassionate, she was a mother whose kindness touched everyone around her.

Mary is the wife of our late uncle Samir Bogdady. She is survived by her 4 daughters and their families:
  • Mimi Boghdady Lunot, Cherine Tucker, Caroline Dennison and Samantha Bogdady DeCastro.
  • And her grandchildren, Julia, Kyle, Joshua, Rachel, Natalie, Brooklyn, Nathan and Gabriella.
  • Along with her Brothers and Sisters in Bellingham and Seattle and her Nephew Tarek Haddad and Neice Nermine Abadir.

Funeral will be on Tuesday, February 17.

Viewing: 11:30-12:30 followed by mass at: 12:30 pm at St Joseph the Worker, 4451 Williams Road, Richmond, BC.

Burial to follow at Victory Memorial : 14831 28 Avenue, Surrey, BC.

Thereafter, there will be a reception at the Victory Memorial Banquet Hall.
