Passing of Mr. Mokhtar Mossaad Gayed

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we offer our sincere condolences to Dr. Alaa Gayed on the passing of his father Mr. Mokhtar Gayed.

Mr. Mokhtar Gayed peacefully passed in Cairo, Egypt late today September 27, 2024. May he rest in peace in the eternal Jerusalem. He is survived by:

His wife: Mrs. Fawzia Milad
His son: Dr Alaa Gayed in Vancouver
Daughter: Gihane Gayed in Cairo
Daughter in law: Erini Mousa
Grandchildren: Marina and Nicole Gayed

The funeral will be held on Saturday, September 28, 2024, at St Mary & St. Mina church Nasr city, Cairo, Egypt.

The family in Canada will receive condolences after liturgy on Sunday, September 29, 2024, at Maddaugh Elementary School, 19405 76 Ave, Surrey, BC, V4N 6C6.
