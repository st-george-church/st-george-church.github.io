Passing of Mr. Samir Aziz Guindi

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we offer our sincere condolences to Mr. Sameh Guindi, Mrs. Nivine Guindi, and grandchildren (Samuel Guindi and Simon Guindi) on the passing of Sameh's father Mr. Samir Aziz Guindi.

Mr. Samir Aziz Guindi tragically passed less than a one month after the passing of his wife in Boston, Massachusetts, USA. The funeral service was held in Boston on Monday September 22, 2024. May he rest in peace in the presence of Our Lord Jesus Christ.

The family will receive condolences after liturgy on Sunday, September 8, 2024 at Maddaugh Elementary School, 19405 76 Ave, Surrey, BC, V4N 6C6.
