Passing of Brigadier General
Magdy Ghattas Salib

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we offer our sincere condolences to Dr. Marian Magdy and her family on the passing of Brigadier General Magdy Ghattas Salib.

Brigadier General Magdy Ghattas Salib is survived by his daughter Dr. Marian Magdy and son-in-law Dr. Amir Ayoub.

The family will receive condolences on Sunday December 8, 2024 after the Divine Liturgy.
