Passing of Dr. Nashaat Magdy Gerges

On behalf of Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we offer our sincere condolences to Nancy Magdy and her family on the passing of her beloved brother Dr. Nashaat Magdy Gerges.

Dr. Nashaat Magdy Gerges departed peacefully on Sunday, December 15, 2024, after living a life of fulfillment and giving from a pure heart to all who knew him.

Dr. Nashaat Magdy Gerges is survived by his wife, Dr. Irini Aziz Nakhla, in Alexandria. He was the son of the late Mr. Magdy Gerges and Mrs. Alice Naguib, and the beloved brother of Nancy Magdy. He was also the cherished uncle of Gabriella Bekhit and Daniel Bekhit.

Upon her return from Egypt, Nancy Magdy will be accepting condolences on Sunday, December 29, 2024, after the Sunday liturgy.
