Passing of Mrs. Mona Gad

It is with great sadness that we announce the sudden passing of Mrs. Mona Gad on May 22, 2024, in Surrey BC.

Mona is survived by her husband Wafik Akladios, son Tamer (Daniela), daughter Tamirane (Michael) and grandchildren Arthanious, Damian and Anneliese.

She is also survived by her brother Moenes in Cairo, brother-in-law Reda Akladios (Fernanda), niece Katherine Akladios and cousins in Egypt and Australia.

The funeral service will be held on May 30, 2024 at 11:30 AM at Saint Mary Coptic Orthodox Church, 12469 104 Avenue, Surrey. The funeral service will be followed by a burial service at Victory Memorial Park, 14831 - 28th Avenue, Surrey.
