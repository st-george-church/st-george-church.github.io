Passing of Deacon
Nabil Riad Sahyoun

It is with deep sadness and heartbreak that we announce the passing of Deacon Dr. Nabil Riad Sahyoun. Dr. Nabil Riad Sahyoun passed away on November 11, 2024 in Vancouver, British Columbia. May his soul rest in peace in the Eternal Jerusalem.

He departed with the hope of resurrection. Dr. Nabil Riad Sahyoun is the husband of the late Mrs. Sanaa Soliman and the father of Antonios, Mariam (Matthew), and Bishoy. He is the brother of the late Mrs. Laila Riad Sahyoun, the late Mr. Nagy Riad Sahyoun, the late Mr. Hany Riad Sahyoun, Mrs. Sonia Riad Sahyoun, and the late Mr. Emad Riad Sahyoun.

May God repose his soul in the paradise of joy and continue to grant His comfort and His peace to his family and friends.

The funeral prayer service and burial will be held on Friday, November 22, 2024.
