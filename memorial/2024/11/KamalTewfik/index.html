Passing of Mr. Kamal Tewfik

It is with profound sadness that we announce the passing of Deacon Kamal Tewfik.

Deacon Kamal Tewfik passed away earlier today in British Columbia. May his soul rest in peace in the eternal Jerusalem. He is survived by his brother, Dr. Sherif Tewfik, a psychiatrist.

Funeral arrangements will be announced shortly.
