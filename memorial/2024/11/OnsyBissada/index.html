Celebrating the life of
Onsy Bissada

On behalf of HG Bishop Mina, Father Bisenty, Father Armia, the Board of Deacons, and the entire church congregation, we offer our sincere condolences to Mrs. Mona Sawiris and her family on the passing of Onsy Bissada.

Onsy Bissada, 79, passed away on November 22, 2024. He is survived by his wife, Mona Sawiris, of 43 years; his children, Marriam (Peter) Ewaida and Youssef Bissada; and his two grandchildren, Leila and Ethan Ewaida, all of whom will never forget the love he brought to their lives.

Psalms 65:4

Blessed is he whom you have chosen and accepted to dwell in your courts forever.
