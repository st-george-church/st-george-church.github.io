Walkathon fundraising event

Dear Parents,

We are thrilled to announce our upcoming walkathon fundraising event organized by the Sunday school classes! This event offers a wonderful opportunity for our children to engage in a fun activity while supporting important projects for our church and Sunday school.

The walkathon will involve children walking from Fort Langley Community Church (8870 Glover Rd) to Derby Reach Regional Park Heritage Area (10716 Allard Crescent, Langley TWP) and back. The one-way distance is 4.7 KM and would take just over one hour. The funds raised will support various church and Sunday school projects.

Event Details:

   Date: Saturday, September 7th
   Time: 10 AM
   Meeting place: Fort Langley Community Church

To participate, please assist your child in gathering donations from friends, family, and neighbors before the event. Donations can be submitted in cash or via e-transfer to donation@stgeorge.ca. All donations should be collected and submitted by Friday, August 30th. A tax receipt will be issued for donations of $20 or more. Download the pledge form here.

When making an e-transfer, please specify that the donation is for the walkathon and include your child's name and grade. For example: Walkathon - John - Grade 4.

On the day of the walkathon, we encourage the children to bring a water bottle and wear comfortable clothing or their Nady T-shirt. After the walk, there will be a BBQ, games, a car wash, and additional activities, making it a day of fun and community spirit.

We also have exciting incentives: the class that raises the most funds will win a pizza party, and the top individual fundraiser will receive a special prize.

Thank you for your support, and we look forward to seeing everyone at the walkathon! Dogs with leash are welcome in the event.
