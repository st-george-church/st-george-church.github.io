We are thrilled to announce our upcoming walkathon fundraising event organized by Sunday school classes! However, everyone is invited and welcome.

This event offers a wonderful opportunity for our children to engage in a fun activity while supporting important projects for our church and Sunday school.

The walkathon will involve children walking from Fort Langley Community Church ( 8870 Glover Rd) to Derby Reach Regional Park Heritage Area (10716 Allard Crescent, Langley TWP) and back. During the walk, we will be holding St. Mary and St. George pictures while chanting our Coptic Christian songs. Funds raised will support various church and Sunday school projects.

Event Details:

   Date: Saturday, September 7th
   Time: 10 AM
   Meeting place: Fort Langley Community Church

After the walk, there will be a BBQ, ice-cream, donuts, games, car wash, and additional activities, making it a day of fun and community spirit.

To participate, please assist your child in gathering donations from friends, family, and neighbors before the event. Donations can be submitted in cash or via e-transfer to donation@stgeorge.ca. A tax receipt will be issued for donations of $20 or more. When making an e-transfer, please specify that the donation is for the walkathon.

Thank you for your support, and we look forward to seeing everyone at the walkathon! Dogs with leash are welcome in the event.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact Abouna Armia at 778-865-4377, Souzy Khalil at 604-781-0154, or Yasmine Bishai at 778-554-7194.
