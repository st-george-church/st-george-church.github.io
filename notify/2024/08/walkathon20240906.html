Walkathon Fundraising Event Tomorrow

All are Welcome
(young, old, and every age in between)

This event offers a wonderful opportunity for our children to engage in a fun activity while supporting important projects for our church.

The walkathon will involve participants walking from Fort Langley Community Church ( 8870 Glover Rd) to Derby Reach Regional Park Heritage Area (10716 Allard Crescent, Langley TWP) and back. During the walk, we will be holding St. Mary and St. George pictures while chanting our Coptic Christian songs. Funds raised will support various church projects.

Event Details
Date: Saturday, September 7th (tomorrow)
Time:10 AM
Meeting place:Fort Langley Community Church

After the walk, there will be a BBQ, ice-cream, donuts, games, car wash, and additional activities, making it a day of fun and community spirit.

Please assist your child in gathering donations from friends, family, and neighbors. Donations can be submitted in cash or via e-transfer to donation@stgeorge.ca. A tax receipt will be issued for donations of $20 or more. When making an e-transfer, please specify that the donation is for the walkathon.

Thank you for your support, and we look forward to seeing everyone at the walkathon! Dogs with leash are welcome in the event.
