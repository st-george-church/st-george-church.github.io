New land blessed by Pope Tawadros

On a recent visit to Egypt, Fr. Armia visited Pope Tawadros and showed him the plans for our new church. Pope Tawadros was very pleased with the plans and blessed the new land at 18555-88th Avenue in Surrey. He also gave us a special blessing for the new church.
