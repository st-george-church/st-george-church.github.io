"Then I said to them, 'You see the trouble we are in: Jerusalem lies in ruins, and its gates have been burned with fire. Come, let us rebuild the wall of Jerusalem, and we will no longer be in disgrace.'" - Nehemiah 2:17

Dear Beloved Members of Our Church Family,

I pray this message finds you in peace and grace.

Since the burn down of our church building, we have journeyed together through prayer, fellowship, and resilience to re-build St. George Church.

Today, we stand at a pivotal moment - a moment to rise and rebuild the house of the Lord, just as Nehemiah once called his people to do.

The vision for our new church home is not just about bricks and mortar. It is about restoring a sacred space where we gather to worship, celebrate, mourn, and grow in faith.

It is about creating a lasting legacy for generations to come and, we are just a couple of weeks away from submitting our revised building application permit.

To bring this vision to life, we humbly ask for your continued and increased support through increased monthly donations. Every contribution - no matter the size - is a vital part of this mission.

Your generosity fuels the foundation of our rebuilding efforts and sustains the many commitments we have made to architects, contractors, surveyors, and community outreach.

We know that times are challenging, and we ask only what each heart feels called to give. If you are able to increase your monthly offering, even modestly, it will make a profound difference on our financial strain.

Together, we can ensure that our church rises again - stronger, more welcoming, and filled with the Spirit.

Let us rebuild our church together.

Monthly donations can be set as follows:

  • e-Transfers to donation@stgeorge.ca
  • Credit card thru PayPal (Link at https://stgeorge.ca)
  • Wire transfers or Direct Debit - please connect with Church treasurer.
  • Personal Post Dated Cheques

Also, the church is now accepting 2-3 years Loans from members of the congregation in denominations of $10,000 or multiples of the same at the very attractive interest rate of 3.5% per annum (to be paid out or donated and receipted).

Your kindness and generosity is greatly appreciated.

Thank you and God bless.
