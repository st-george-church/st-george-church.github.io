The Embassy of Egypt in Ottawa is pleased to announce a consular mission to Vancouver, taking place on October 12th and 13th, 2025.

Location:

Vancouver Airport Marriot Hotel
7571 Westminster Hwy,Richmond, BC

Available Services Include:

  • Renewal of Egyptian National ID cards
  • Issuance and renewal of Egyptian passports
  • Issuance of Powers of Attorney
  • Other consular services for Egyptian nationals

For a complete list of services offered during this mission, please refer to the Embassy's official Facebook post to review allowed consular services and check the attached documents.
