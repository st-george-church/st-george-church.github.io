Parking Information:

There are 70 parking spots available at Walnut Grove Lutheran Church, on a first-come / first-served basis. Due to the anticipated attendance, we kindly encourage carpooling or using alternative transportation when possible.

Parking Guidelines:

Use Only Assigned Parking Spots: Please park only in marked spots within the church lot.

Do Not Block Fire Lanes or Emergency Access Areas: Vehicles obstructing these zones may be subject to towing or fines.

Surrounding Area Parking: Parking in the general vicinity of the church is permitted, as long as vehicles are not obstructing traffic, driveways, or private property.

Follow Ushers' Directions: Our ushers will assist in guiding traffic and helping you park safely and efficiently.

RCMP Presence: While the RCMP is not on site, they are typically active in the surrounding area. Compliance with parking and traffic rules is appreciated to avoid any issues.

Plan Ahead: Please allow extra time for parking and entry to the church to ensure a calm and timely arrival.

Important Notes:

Ushers Are Here to Help: Please cooperate with our ushers, who are volunteering to ensure everyone's safety, comfort, and a smooth flow of entry and exit.
