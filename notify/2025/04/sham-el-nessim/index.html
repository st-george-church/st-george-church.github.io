Where: Peace Arch Provincial Park, 16756 Beach Rd, Surrey, BC V3S 9R6.
When: The event will start at 2:30 PM and end at 7:30 PM.

  • BBQ chicken
  • Lasagna
  • Renga
  • Caesar Salad
  • Hot Dogs
  • and more ...

To assist in covering our expenses, including hall rental, the cost will be as follows:

Age Cost
0-4 years Free
5-12 years $20
13 years and older $25

Please register by contacting and paying in cash to Manal Tanyous (778-223-4990) or Shady Moussa (778-551-2787) at the church.

We look forward to having a great fellowship time.
