Parking Information

There are 70 parking spots available at Walnut Grove Lutheran Church on a first-come, first-served basis. Due to anticipated attendance, carpooling or alternative transportation is encouraged when possible.

Parking Guidelines

Use only assigned parking spots: Please park only in clearly marked spaces within the church lot. Do Not Block Fire Lanes or Emergency Access Areas. Vehicles blocking these areas may be subject to towing or fines.

Surrounding area parking: Street parking is permitted in the surrounding area, provided vehicles do not obstruct traffic, driveways, or private property.

Follow ushers' directions: Our ushers will assist with traffic flow and parking to ensure safety and efficiency.

RCMP Presence

While RCMP officers will not be on site, they are typically active in the surrounding area. Please comply with all parking and traffic regulations.

Plan Ahead
