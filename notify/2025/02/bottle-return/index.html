Brothers and Sisters in Christ,

We are excited to announce a fundraising initiative for our new church! We will be collecting bottles through Return-It. Our treasurer will have full access to the account and will periodically transfer funds to the church bank account.

How to Participate:

  1. Collect your beverage containers in a clear (transparent or blue) bag.
  2. Find the nearest Return-It Express location here.
  3. At the depot, print a label for your bags using the account number 604-300-4235. Attach the label to the transparent bag.
  4. Drop off your bag! No sorting necessary.

View this brief brochure for additional information.

Alternative Option:

You can bring your transparent bag to the church on Sundays and give it to Magued Ghabour or Mina Fahim. They will gladly take it to Return-It for you.

Accepted Containers:

  • Aluminum Cans
  • Plastic Bottles
  • Gable Top Containers (e.g., paper-based milk and juice containers)
  • Drink Boxes
  • Glass Bottles
  • Bi-Metal Containers (two layers of different metals joined together that can be recycled)
  • Pouches
  • Bag-in-a-Box Containers (e.g., cereal packaging)
  • Liquor Plastic Bottles
  • Liquor Glass Bottles
  • Beer Bottles
  • Kegs
