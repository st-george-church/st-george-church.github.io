Parking Availability

Full Parking Lot Procedure

If the parking lot is full and no spots are available:

  • Drivers may briefly drop off family members or passengers at the church entrance

Parking Rules

  • Park only in clearly marked parking spaces

  • Improperly parked vehicles may be subject to towing or fines

  • Snow is expected during this season

  • Be mindful of icy conditions, limited visibility, and pedestrians

Street Parking

  • Vehicles must not obstruct traffic, private property, sidewalks, or driveways

  • Kindly follow their directions, as they are serving to maintain safety and order

  • Please comply with all traffic and parking regulations

Plan Ahead

Please allow extra time for travel, parking, and safe entry, especially during winter road conditions.
