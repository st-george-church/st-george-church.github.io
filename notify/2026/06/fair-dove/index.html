Dear Church Family,

We are pleased to introduce Fair Dove, a specialized service designed to support individuals with special needs and their families through a safe, structured, and welcoming worship environment.

Fair Dove is committed to fostering inclusion, dignity, and belonging for every participant. This service provides a calm, sensory-friendly setting that incorporates gentle worship, simplified teaching, visual supports, music, and opportunities for meaningful social connection. Our goal is to create a supportive space where individuals can engage at their own pace while growing in faith and experiencing a sense of community.

Guided by the principle, "Welcome one another as Christ has welcomed you, for the glory of God” (Romans 15:7), this service prioritizes compassion, accessibility, and individualized support to ensure all participants feel respected, valued, and cared for. Families interested in participating are encouraged to complete the registration form.
