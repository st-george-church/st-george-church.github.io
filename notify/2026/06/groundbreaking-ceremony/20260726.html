Dear beloved congregation,

By the grace of God, you are warmly invited to attend the Groundbreaking Ceremony for St. George Coptic Orthodox Church.

His Grace Bishop Mina, Hegumen Fr. Bisenty Abdel-Messih Gerges and Fr. Armia Aziz Gerges cordially invite you to share in this historic blessing as we celebrate the groundbreaking of our new church building.

We look forward to celebrating this historic day with you and your family.

In Christ,
St. George Coptic Orthodox Church
