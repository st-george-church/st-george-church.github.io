Remembering July 19, 2021

On Sunday, July 19, 2026, we mark five years since our church was destroyed. By God’s grace, our community has remained united, steadfast, and faithful throughout every challenge.

Since that day, we have purchased a 5‑acre parcel of land, secured church zoning, and obtained all necessary permits from the City of Surrey to begin construction. Now, with gratitude and hope, we prepare for the next milestone.

On Sunday, July 26, 2026, we will lay the cornerstone of our new church in the blessed presence of His Grace Bishop Mina, Fr. Bisenty, and Fr. Armia. This moment marks the beginning of rebuilding our spiritual home.

We thank God for sustaining our congregation and allowing us to continue our services without interruption. We pray that the Lord will guide and strengthen us as we work toward completing the construction of our new church.
