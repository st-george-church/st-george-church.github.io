Remembering July 19, 2021

On Sunday July 19, 2026 it will be exactly five years since our Church was destroyed. We have since successfully purchased a 5-acre piece of land, zoned it for church, and obtained the necessary permits from the City Of Surrey to start construction. On Sunday July 26, 2026 we will be laying the cornerstone for the new Church in the presence of HG Bishop Mina, Fr. Bisenty, and Fr. Armia. We thank God for keeping our congregation together and helping us continue with uninterrupted church services and pray that The Lord helps us complete the construction of our new Church.
