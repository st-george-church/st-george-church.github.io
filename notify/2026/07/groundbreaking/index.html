Dear Members of the Congregation,

We are excited to gather on Sunday, July 26 for the groundbreaking ceremony of our new church. This is an important milestone in the life of our church, and we look forward to celebrating it together.

Please plan to arrive between 12:30 to 12:45 p.m. to allow sufficient time for parking and to ensure the ceremony can begin at 1pm sharp. Upon arrival, please follow the directions of the ushers and volunteers who will be assisting with parking and guiding attendees throughout the site.

Please keep the following in mind to ensure this event is as successful as possible:

  • Parking will be limited due to ongoing construction. We strongly encourage everyone to carpool whenever possible to help ensure adequate parking for all attendees. Families who took 2 + cars to church, please ensure you only come in 1 car to the event.
  • Please be prepared to stand during the event; seating will be very limited and chairs will be reserved primarily for elderly attendees and those who require them.
  • Please wear comfortable footwear suitable for uneven ground. High heels and similar footwear are not recommended.
  • Parents and guardians must closely supervise their children at all times.
  • There is only 1 construction washroom on site so please plan accordingly.
  • Forecast is calling for rain on Sunday so please bring umbrellas with you.
  • Entrance and exit from the site is by right turn only; right turn from 88 Avenue westbound, and departure will be by right turn onto 88 Avenue westbound as well (please see the attached photo).

Thank you for your cooperation and understanding. We look forward to celebrating this special occasion together and taking the first step toward our new church home.

If you have not yet registered for the event, please click here ( Groundbreaking Ceremony Registration ) to complete your registration. We encourage all members of the congregation to attend.

Thank you for your participation in this memorable event.

St. George Board & Planning Committee
