Dear Beloved Congregation,

Greetings in Christ! We hope this message finds you well. We are writing to share the wonderful updates from the quarterly New Church Project presentation delivered on Sunday, May 24, 2026.

Design Approval - A Major Milestone!

We are thrilled to announce that after completing an extensive list of city reviews and requirements, including a final plumbing issue resolved this week, the City of Surrey has officially approved our design and all related submissions. This represents countless hours of work and brings us significantly closer to breaking ground.

Financial Update - God's Abundant Blessings

By God's grace and your incredible generosity, we have raised $850,000 toward our building project! Even more encouraging is that the number of families contributing has significantly increased, demonstrating our unity and shared vision to rebuild our spiritual home. We have also set aside funds to cover operating expenses, including this year's property tax.

Next Steps - Building Permit & Construction

We are now ready to begin construction this summer, starting with:

Site Preparation: $300,000
Foundation: $825,000
Total: $1.125 million

To secure our building permit, we need to:

1. Finalize the servicing agreement (already in progress)
2. Pay a refundable deposit of $890,000 to the City

This deposit is required for our work on city roads and connections to water and drainage systems. Once the work is completed and inspected, the City will refund this amount in full.

Loan Drive - Your Support Needed

Following the success of our loan drive three years ago (which raised $550,000), we are launching a new loan drive with a goal of $890,000. Here are the details:

Collection Dates: June 7 and July 5 (after liturgies)
Minimum Loan Amount: $5,000 or more
Terms: 2-year or 3-year non-cashable loans
Interest Rate: 0.25% higher than current bank GIC rates
Contact: Our Secretary and Treasurer will be available to collect cheques

July 19 - A Date of Significance

We are targeting July 19, 2026 as our deadline to complete this loan drive and pay the deposit. This date marks five years since the fire that destroyed our church. What a blessing it would be to transform this day of loss into a day of hope and renewal!

If we meet this goal, we will celebrate with:

  • A groundbreaking ceremony
  • The cornerstone prayer
  • A commemorative event marking the beginning of construction

These moments will symbolize not just construction, but renewal, perseverance, and the faith that has sustained us through every trial.

Looking Forward with Faith

As King David wrote in Psalm 122:1, "I was glad for those who said to me, 'we will go into the house of the Lord.'" As we continue hoping and praying for the day when we take our first steps into our new church, let us remind each other to focus on the destination, not the duration.

If you are able to participate in the loan drive, please plan to attend on June 7 or July 5. If you have questions or would like to discuss your contribution, please reach out to our Secretary or Treasurer.

Thank you for your continued prayers, support, and unwavering faith. Together, by God's grace, we are building not just a structure, but a home for our spiritual family.

In Christ's love,

Board of Deacons
St. George Coptic Orthodox Church
