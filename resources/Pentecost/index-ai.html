Feast of Pentecost on Sunday, May 31, 2026

We will celebrate the Feast of Pentecost on Sunday, May 31, 2026. Please note the following schedule updates on that day:
  • Divine Liturgy will begin early at 8:00 AM (instead of 8:30 AM)
  • Prostration Prayers (Sagda) will take place from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM
The Apostles' Fast begins on the next day (Monday, June 1) and concludes on the Feast of the Apostles (Martyrdom of St. Peter & St. Paul) on Sunday, July 12.
