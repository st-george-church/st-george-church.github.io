Media Coverage: St. George Coptic Orthodox Church Fire

On July 19, 2021, St. George Coptic Orthodox Church in Surrey, BC was destroyed by fire, just days after an attempted arson at the same location. The incident received widespread media coverage across Canada and internationally. This page compiles news articles, videos, and other coverage related to the destruction of our church building, subsequent investigation, and rebuilding efforts.
