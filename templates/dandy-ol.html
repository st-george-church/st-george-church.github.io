  1. Commemoration of St. Mary
    On Monday, December 29, 2025, we will pray Kiahk Tasbeha starting at 7:00 PM, followed by the Divine Liturgy in commemoration of St. Mary.
  2. New Year's Eve Celebration
    Our New Year's Eve program begins at 7:00 PM on December 31, 2025, at Walnut Grove Lutheran Church. The evening will include Asheya, a Sunday School Celebration, and Tasbeha, continuing until midnight.
  3. Divine Liturgy for the New Year
    Hegumen Fr. Bisenty will pray the Divine Liturgy during the first hours of the New Year, from 12:00 AM to 2:30 AM.
  4. Bible Studies Update
    Please note that there will be no online Bible Studies on January 1 or January 7, 2026.
